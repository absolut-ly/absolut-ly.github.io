About the Center

For the community

The Center was started to help the community in any way possible. We now offer free internet use, warm and comfortable space and friendship, and other various events to help meet the needs of our town.


Drinks and Snacks

We offer a wide variety of drinks and snacks.


Computer/Internet Usage

The Center offers free wi-fi internet access. We also have several computers available to use free of charge. (We ask that guests observe a 30 minute time limit when others are waiting to use the computers.) Printing solutions are also available

Our Mission- Why We Are Here

As residents of Surry we face numerous challenges in our community. Young people are in need of positive activities and strong role models. Many people are struggling with financial problems, relationship issues, and loneliness. Divisions of race and background fragment our community. That is why we are here
